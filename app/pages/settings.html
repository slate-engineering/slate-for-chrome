FAQs

My upload is complete, but it still shows the uploading bar.
Something may have went wrong when updating your local database after a successful upload.
Click the reset button below to fix this (it will not affect any uploads).


Where is my data stored?
Your data from the Slate for Chrome app is only stored locally and never shared with Slate or any third-party apps.
Since it's stored locally, it's recomended to clear your upload history on occasion.

What is a direct upload?
A simple, one-click upload that adds an image to your primary API account, not to a specific slate.

Do uploads run in the background?
Yes! After the upload starts, you can close the app and continue browsing.

I found a bug, how can I report it?
Awesome! Feel free to file a new issue with a respective title and description on our
Github repo with your bug report!
